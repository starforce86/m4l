  • Navigate to Chapter page

Step #2: Get Live

Check List

  • Update About Us
    • Chapters: Edit Chapter: Content - Add chapter specific info, chapter leadership, committees, school board info, etc.
  • Add Payment Link
    • Chapters: Edit Chapter: Sponsors - right click the text "donate to this chapter" click link, and paste your payment link in the URL box.
    • Chapters: Edit Chapter: External Payment Link - paste your payment link in the box
  • Edit Join Form
    • Chapters: Customize App Fields - update district options, separated by commas
    • Chapters: Set/Update Prices - update pricing for regular membership dues
  • Upload Files
    • Apps: File. File: Add a File - At a minimum add your code of conduct, but I recommend adding your bylaws and any other documents relevant to your chapter
  • At this point, email Jade@MomsForLiberty.org, say you're ready to go active and attach your newsletter/membership list.
    • After you membership list is uploaded, you'll need to add your Chapter Leadership.
      • Chapters: Edit Chapter: Add officer - add your core four (Chair, V Chair, Secretary, Treasuer)
